Pancake Easy Recipe


Ingredients:-

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil
    plus a little extra for frying
  • lemon wedges to serve (optional)
  • caster sugar to serve (optional)

Method:-

  1. step

    2. Put 100g plain flour
    2 large eggs, 300ml milk
    1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil
    and a pinch of salt into a bowl
    or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter.

  2. step

    3. Set aside for 30 mins to rest
    if you have time, or start cooking
    straight away.

  3. step

    4. Set a medium frying pan or crÃªpe pan
    over a medium heat and carefully wipe it
    with some oiled kitchen paper.

  4. step

    5. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min
    on each side until golden, keeping them
    warm in a low oven as you go.

  5. step

    6. Serve with lemon wedges and caster sugar,
    or your favourite filling. Once cold,
    you can layer the pancakes between baking parchment,
    then wrap in cling film and freeze for up to 2 months.
